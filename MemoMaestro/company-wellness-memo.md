# MEMORANDUM

**TO:** All Employees

**FROM:** Human Resources & Employee Engagement Teams

**DATE:** May 15, 2023

**SUBJECT:** Launching Fit4Success — Our New Employee Wellness Program

---

## Dear Colleagues,

We are pleased to announce the launch of **Fit4Success**, a comprehensive new employee wellness program designed to support every aspect of your health and well-being. Fit4Success will officially kick off on **June 1, 2023**, and we invite each of you to take part in building a healthier, more energized workplace.

---

## Program Objectives

Fit4Success has been developed with the following goals in mind:

- **Promote physical and mental well-being** across all levels of the organization
- **Reduce stress** by providing accessible tools, resources, and support
- **Increase productivity** by fostering healthier habits that lead to greater focus, energy, and job satisfaction

We believe that when our people thrive, our organization thrives.

---

## Program Components

Fit4Success is built around four key pillars, each designed to address a different dimension of wellness:

### 1. On-Site Fitness Classes
A rotating schedule of instructor-led fitness classes will be offered at our on-site facilities, including:
- Yoga and stretching sessions
- High-intensity interval training (HIIT)
- Pilates and core strengthening
- Walking and running groups

Classes will be available before work, during lunch, and after work hours to accommodate a variety of schedules.

### 2. Healthy Meal Options
Our cafeteria and break rooms will feature an expanded selection of nutritious meals and snacks, including:
- Fresh salads, grain bowls, and lean protein options
- Seasonal fruit and vegetable selections
- Clearly labeled nutritional information to help you make informed choices

### 3. Mental Health Resources
Your mental well-being is just as important as your physical health. Fit4Success includes:
- Access to confidential counseling services through our Employee Assistance Program (EAP)
- Guided meditation and mindfulness sessions
- Stress management toolkits and self-assessment resources

### 4. Wellness Workshops
Throughout the year, we will host expert-led workshops on a range of wellness topics, such as:
- Nutrition and meal planning
- Sleep hygiene and recovery
- Financial wellness and stress reduction
- Ergonomics and workplace health
- Work-life balance strategies

---

## Gym & Wellness Center Partnerships

To extend your wellness journey beyond the office, we have partnered with **select local gyms and wellness centers** to offer exclusive discounted memberships to all employees. Details on participating locations and pricing will be available on the company intranet upon enrollment.

---

## Incentives for Participation

We want to make your wellness journey rewarding. Participants in Fit4Success will enjoy the following incentives:

| Incentive | Details |
|---|---|
| **Fitness Trackers** | All enrolled participants will receive a complimentary fitness tracker to monitor activity levels, set goals, and track progress. |
| **Wellness Points** | Earn points by attending classes, completing workshops, and hitting personal milestones. Points can be redeemed for gift cards, extra PTO, and wellness merchandise. |
| **Prize Drawings** | Monthly drawings for participants who meet activity thresholds, with prizes including spa packages, premium gym memberships, and wellness retreat vouchers. |

---

## How to Enroll

Enrollment is simple and can be completed in just a few minutes:

1. **Log in** to the company intranet at [intranet.company.com/fit4success](http://intranet.company.com/fit4success)
2. **Navigate** to the Fit4Success registration page under the "Employee Programs" section
3. **Complete** the online registration form, including your wellness interests and preferred class times
4. **Submit** your enrollment before **May 28, 2023** to ensure you are ready for the June 1 launch

If you experience any issues with the registration process, please reach out to the HR Help Desk.
