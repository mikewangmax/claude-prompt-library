# Similes for Joyful, Contagious Laughter

Here are some similes to describe a person's laughter that is joyful and contagious:

1. Her laughter rang out like a cascade of silver bells tumbling down a hillside, impossible to hear without smiling yourself.

2. His laugh was like a spark landing in dry kindling — the moment it started, everyone around him caught fire with it.

3. Their laughter spread through the room like sunlight pouring through a suddenly opened curtain, brightening every face it touched.

4. She laughed like a brook spilling over smooth stones — bright, musical, and so full of natural delight that passersby couldn't help but stop and listen.

5. His laughter was like the first warm breeze of spring, sweeping through a crowd and thawing even the most frozen expressions into grins.

6. Her laugh erupted like a bottle of champagne uncorked at a party — effervescent, overflowing, and instantly lifting the mood of everyone nearby.

7. Their laughter rolled through the room like a wave at a stadium, each person picking it up and carrying it forward until the whole place was roaring.

8. He laughed like a child discovering bubbles for the first time — pure, unguarded, and so infectiously gleeful that the adults around him forgot to be serious.

9. Her laughter was like a song stuck in your head, the kind that replays itself so insistently that you find yourself humming along before you realize it.

10. His laugh burst out like popcorn in a hot pan — sudden, rapid, impossible to contain, and setting off a chain reaction in everyone within earshot.

11. Their laughter was like a yawn in reverse — instead of spreading drowsiness, it sent jolts of joy rippling outward through every person in the room.

12. She laughed like a match struck in a dark room, and suddenly everyone could see the humor they had been stumbling around in the dark to find.

13. His laughter was like the drumbeat at a carnival — rhythmic, irresistible, and before you knew it, your whole body was moving along with it.

14. Her laugh tumbled out like a jar of marbles spilling across a hardwood floor — bright, bouncing, scattering in every direction, and utterly impossible to ignore.

15. Their laughter was like the smell of fresh-baked cookies drifting from a kitchen — warm, inviting, and capable of drawing people in from rooms away with no effort at all.
